The Legacy : TROIKA


IEEE DTU, one of the most active student branch of IEEE proudly presents it's annual technical fest TROIKA 2018. Troika, which is Russian for "a set of three", represents the three pillars of engineering: Approach, Theory and Practice. With an array of competitive events covering all aspects of technical aptitude, Troika is one of the most eagerly awaited Techfests of North India and attracts participation from all over the country. Continuing its rich legacy, Troika 2018 aspires to be an exhibition of, and a platform for young engineering talent.


Promoting Diversity in Technology


The events in association with IEEE Women in Engineering, DTU include programming, robotics and electronics challenges which aim to promote love for innovation and technology, especially among women by having special prizes for the best girl team and participant respectively.
