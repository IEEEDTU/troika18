About Radix

RADIX, the most awaited robotics-based event is back in Troika 2018. This phenomenal event provides an opportunity to people enthusiastic in the field of robotics to showcase their talents. The event is the ultimate fusion of hardware and programming.

Welcome to the Ultimate Robotics Challenge, where you will get a sneak peek into the Age Of The Robots. So grab your tools, fuse code and hardware, and meet us in the arena.
