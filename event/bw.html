About Brainwave

We’re living in a world made of ICs, microprocessors, transistors, capacitors, diodes and what not. Everything running our world is powered by the force of electronics.

If you think that electronics is your passion and you can think beyond the boundaries to innovate something different, then come to Brainwave 2018 this February. A hardware design challenge that will test you to the limits, that will not require you to push yourself but also take your thinking to the next level.

Showcase your hardware skills in the competition made to sieve out the best. Dare to see the Ultimate? Do come.

Details

Brainwave’18 is divided into two parts:
• A predefined hardware challenge.
• Digital system design and Verilog based event.
