About Design-o-Thon

"There are three responses to a piece of design- Yes, no, Wow! Wow is the one to aim for".
Hey folks, what's up? Living in this world are you creative enough to take anything around you and make use of it? Yes we all are! So,why not design and brind innovation to the way we see things, bring beauty and order into this seeming randomness

Do you want to show off your inner artist to the world? Is that beautiful brain of your stick in a place where it is not recognized? portray your imagination to us on the digital canvas of your computer screen. Creativity that takes courage and heart is ultimate source of courage.
So pour your heart into a piece of art and present in design-o-thon! Be it animation,web page designing,CAD designs or just a simple art-work, all is appreciated here

Details

TROIKA 2018 comes up with an event, Design-o-thon which is an amalgamation of photoshop, animation, solidworks and creativity. The event has four subcategories :

• Design-O-Thon
Bring in The Graphic Designer Within You

• Ani-mission
Gear Up to Showcase Your Animation Skills

• 3D-Pro
Showcase Your 3D Designing Skills

• Sketch-It
Unleash Your Creativity on Paper
