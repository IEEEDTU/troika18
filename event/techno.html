Platform Your Research.

About Technovision

Technovision, the technical research paper presentation competition of Troika 2018, tests your impeccable inclination in research and exploration as well as your efficiency in presentation skills. Technovision provides an unparalleled opportunity for people with scientifically oriented minds, to platform their extensive research in fields varying from Electronics, Electrical and Computers to Bio-Technology and Chemistry. If college labs infuse thrill, excitement and curiosity in your brains, if you possess that farsighted vision in technology, if you want to fill this world with innovations and inventions, then Technovision is surely the event for you. Calling all the future Einsteins, Teslas and Darwins to present your research and win accolades!

Details

Technovision not only provides you with an opportunity to showcase your research, but also gives a chance to publish your paper in a renowned journal. The event has four categories :

Athena: This includes research papers from streams like Computer Science, Information Technology, Software Engineering and related.

Hermes: This includes research papers from streams like Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Communications Engineering, Instrumentation and related.

Hephaestus: This includes research papers from streams like Mechanical, Production and Automotive Engineering, Civil and Environmental Engineering and related.

Zeus: This includes papers from Applied physics, Applied Chemistry and Chemical Technology, Applied Mathematics and Applied Biology and Biotechnology.

We welcome all the students pursuing Bachelors and Masters (B.Tech/ M.Tech/ B.Sc/ M.Sc/ BS/ MS/ BE/ ME) from various universities of India to grab this opportunity and participate.
